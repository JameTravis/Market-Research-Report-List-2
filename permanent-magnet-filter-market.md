<p><p>The Permanent Magnet Filter Market comprises two types: SMS Type and MS Type. The SMS Type is a self-cleaning filter that employs a magnetic material to capture ferromagnetic particles from the liquid or gas stream. It uses a rotating magnetic drum to effectively separate and remove contaminants. On the other hand, the MS Type is a magnetic separator that utilizes powerful permanent magnets to attract and collect ferrous particles. It provides continuous and efficient separation by diverting the contaminated fluid flow, allowing for easy removal of impurities. Both types offer efficient filtration solutions for various industries requiring contaminant removal.</p></p>
