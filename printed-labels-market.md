<p><p>The printed labels market consists of various types of labels used in different industries. Wet glued labels are applied using adhesive and are commonly used in industries such as beverage and pharmaceuticals. Pressure-sensitive labels adhere to surfaces upon contact and are versatile, used in many sectors. Shrink sleeves are heat-activated labels that conform to the shape of the container. Stretch sleeves provide a 360-degree coverage and are suitable for irregularly shaped objects. Pre-gummed labels have an adhesive backing activated by moisture. In-mould labels are applied during the manufacturing process. Other types include self-adhesive, die-cut, and barcode labels.</p></p>
