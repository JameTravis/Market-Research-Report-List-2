<p><p>Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) are additives used in various industries to protect materials from degradation caused by exposure to light. There are three main types of HALS in the market: polymeric, monomeric, and oligomeric. </p><p>Polymeric HALS are larger molecules that provide excellent performance and durability. They offer higher light and thermal stability, making them suitable for applications in harsh environmental conditions. </p><p>Monomeric HALS are smaller molecules that offer good performance and compatibility with different materials. They provide efficient protection but are less durable compared to polymeric HALS.</p><p>Oligomeric HALS are intermediate in size, offering a balance between the performance of polymeric and monomeric HALS. They are versatile and suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
