<p><p>Breathable polypropylene membranes are widely used in various industries for their excellent breathability properties. They can be classified into different types based on their structure: monolayer, double layer, three layers, and others. Monolayer membranes are made up of a single layer of polypropylene, providing basic breathability. Double layer membranes consist of two layers, offering enhanced breathability and durability. Three layer membranes have an additional layer in the middle, providing even better breathability and strength. Other types may include variations in the number or arrangement of layers, offering unique benefits to meet specific requirements in different applications.</p></p>
