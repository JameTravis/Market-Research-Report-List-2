<p><p>Welding and brazing consumables are materials used in the welding and brazing processes to join two or more metal components. Welding consumables include electrodes, wires, and fluxes that melt and bind the metals together, forming a strong joint. On the other hand, brazing consumables consist of filler metals, fluxes, and rods, which melt at a lower temperature to join metals without melting the base materials. Both welding and brazing consumables are crucial for achieving secure and durable metal connections in various industries such as construction, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
