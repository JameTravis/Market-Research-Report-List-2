<p><p>Liquid Applied Membranes (LAM) are waterproofing systems that are spray or brush-applied onto surfaces to create a seamless and durable protective layer. The market types of LAM include Bituminous, Elastomeric, Cementitious, and Others. Bituminous LAMs are made of asphalt and provide excellent waterproofing properties. Elastomeric LAMs are flexible and offer superior resistance to weathering and UV radiation. Cementitious LAMs are made of cement and are commonly used for waterproofing structures that require resistance against high water pressure. The Others category includes specialty LAMs such as polyurethane and epoxy-based membranes.</p></p>
