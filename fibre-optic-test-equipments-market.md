<p><p>The Fibre Optic Test Equipment market includes various types of equipment. Optical Spectrum Analyzers (OSA) are used for precise measurement of optical signals. Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexers (DWDM) enable multiple signals to be transmitted simultaneously over a single fiber. Optical Time Domain Reflectometers (OTDR) help locate faults and measure losses in fiber optic cables. Optical Light Sources (OLS) produce optical signals for testing. Optical Power Meters (OPM) measure the power of optical signals. Optical Loss Test Sets (OLTS) test the overall loss of fiber optic links. Lastly, Radio Frequency over Fiber Test Sets (RFTS) analyze and measure RF signals transmitted over fiber.</p></p>
