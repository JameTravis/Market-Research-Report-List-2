<p><p>A compressor dehumidifier is a device that removes excess moisture from the air using a compressor and refrigerant. <p><p>Compressor dehumidifiers come in different types, such as tower type and rotor type. Tower type dehumidifiers are designed in a vertical shape, enhancing their airflow and cooling efficiency. They usually have a larger capacity, making them suitable for larger spaces. Rotor type dehumidifiers, on the other hand, use a rotating wheel to absorb moisture from the air. These dehumidifiers are typically smaller and more portable, making them suitable for smaller areas or for moving around as needed.</p></p>
