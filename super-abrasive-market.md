<p><p>The super abrasive market consists of three main types: diamond, cubic boron nitride (CBN), and other abrasives. Diamond abrasives are the most widely used type due to their exceptional hardness and superior thermal conductivity. CBN abrasives are known for their high resistance to heat and are typically used for grinding ferrous materials. The other abrasives category includes materials like boron nitride, which are used in specific applications where diamond or CBN abrasives may not be suitable. These three types cater to different needs within the super abrasive market by offering varying levels of hardness, heat resistance, and versatility.</p></p>
