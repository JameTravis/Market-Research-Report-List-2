<p><p>The Gigabit capable Passive Optical Networks GPON Equipment market consists of three main types: </p><p>1) FTTH/O (Fiber to the Home/Office): This type focuses on providing high-speed internet access directly to homes or offices through fiber optic cables.</p><p>2) FTTB+LAN (Fiber to the Building+Local Area Network): Here, the fiber optic cables are extended up to the building, and then the internet connection is distributed among multiple users through a local area network (LAN).</p><p>3) FTTB+DSL (Fiber to the Building+Digital Subscriber Line): In this type, the fiber optic cables reach the building, but the internet connection is distributed using DSL technology, which utilizes traditional copper phone lines.</p><p>These types of GPON equipment cater to different scenarios and requirements for delivering gigabit-capable internet access.</p></p>
