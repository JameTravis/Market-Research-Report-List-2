<p><p>Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) is a widely used analytical technique in the field of chemistry, biochemistry, and pharmaceuticals. It is used to separate and identify various chemical compounds in a mixture. The developing tank is an essential component in TLC, designed to facilitate the separation process.</p><p>A TLC developing tank is a container used to accommodate the TLC plate, which holds the sample mixture being analyzed. The developing tank is responsible for creating an optimal environment for the separation of compounds on the TLC plate. It typically consists of a chamber with a lid, solvent chamber, and a plate rack. The solvent chamber holds the solvent system required for the separation, while the plate rack holds the TLC plates in a vertical position for uniform development.</p><p>The global Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) Developing Tank Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period.
