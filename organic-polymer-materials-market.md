<p><p>The organic polymer materials market consists of two main types: thermoplastic polymer materials and thermosetting polymer materials. </p><p>Thermoplastic polymer materials have a linear or branched structure that can be melted and re-melted multiple times without undergoing significant chemical changes. They have good flexibility and are commonly used in applications such as packaging, automotive parts, and consumer goods. </p><p>On the other hand, thermosetting polymer materials have a crosslinked structure that cannot be melted once they are cured. They have high strength, stiffness, and heat resistance, making them suitable for applications such as electronic components, automotive parts, and construction materials.</p></p>
