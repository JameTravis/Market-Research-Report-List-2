<p><p>The Ventilation Test Device market comprises various types of devices used for measuring and evaluating ventilation systems. Air Velocity Meters or Anemometers are devices used to measure the speed of airflow. Manometers/Micromanometers measure pressure differences to assess airflow. Leakage Testers are employed to identify any leaks in the ventilation system. Air Capture Hoods are devices used to measure and capture the airflow from diffusers or grilles. These devices play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and effective ventilation in various settings, such as buildings, industries, and healthcare facilities.</p></p>
